How do we achieve role clarity?

Provide the employee with a clear overview of their responsibilities and how their work contributes to team and company goals.

Ensure the employee understands their primary responsibilities, performance expectations and what success in the role looks like. Considerations such as how long the employee has been in their role, how long they have been at Xero, and their overall professional tenure can be helpful in assessing whether the employee fully understands the expectations of their role.

Ensure that performance is a regular topic of conversation by providing coaching and feedback in 1:1 conversations.

Drive accountability in setting clear & measurable goals.

Provide a clear articulation of the capability framework. Ensure that the employee understands what performing in each of the capability areas looks like, and where their strengths and opportunities lie within the capability framework.
